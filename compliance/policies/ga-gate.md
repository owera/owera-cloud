# GA Gate Policy (T13.4)

**Owner:** Product Management (PM workstream)
**Approvers:** PM + Tech Lead + Rodrigo Recio (Owera Software Ltda)
**Review cadence:** quarterly post-GA; immediately on any gate breach.

## Purpose

Define the objective gates that move **Owera Agentic** from **V0 (private beta)** → **V1 (public GA)**. These gates protect customers from premature exposure to immature operations and protect the team from announcing GA before the platform can sustain it.

The gates are gating *only* — meeting all of them is necessary but not sufficient; the final GA call sits with the three approvers above. The gates are also checked at every quarterly review to confirm the platform still meets the GA bar; sustained drift downgrades the offering to "limited beta" until remediation lands.

## Gates

### G1 — Paying customers

- **Threshold:** ≥3 paying customers under signed MSA, each running at least one billed job per week for the trailing 30 days.
- **Why:** Demonstrates the catalogue ramp + onboarding flow + billing work end-to-end against external usage, not just synthetic load.
- **Evidence:** Stripe dashboard report + audit log query (`tenant_id` × `job.submit` count over 30 d).

### G2 — SLA met

- **Threshold:** ≥95% of jobs in the trailing 30 days completed within the SLA window declared by the SKU's `Pricing.SLA.MaxLatencySeconds`.
- **Why:** GA is a promise; sustained SLA breaches at V0 are a Phase-2 hardening signal, not a green light to publicise.
- **Evidence:** `internal/metrics` rollup + `compliance/audit-controls/evidence/CC4.1-monitoring.md` query.
- **SKU-specific carveouts:** if a single SKU is below the bar, GA may still proceed for the remaining SKUs; the breaching SKU stays beta-only until it recovers.

### G3 — Zero cross-tenant audit events

- **Threshold:** Zero rows in `audit_log` where `tenant_id_from_route != tenant_id_from_context` over the trailing 30 days, AND the WS-15 cross-tenant attack-path test (`api/tests/cross_tenant_test.go`) is part of the required CI gate.
- **Why:** Tenant isolation is the single largest legal/contractual blast radius; one leak post-GA is a customer notification + ANPD/LGPD posture problem.
- **Evidence:** CI pass record + audit-log query.

### G4 — Operational drill cadence

- **Threshold:** At least one PagerDuty drill (T19.6) executed within the trailing 90 days, with a post-mortem committed under `compliance/runbooks/incidents/`. Worker-fleet outage drill counts; status-page drill counts.
- **Why:** Without practiced incident response, the first real outage is the first time the team runs the runbook — too late for paying customers.
- **Evidence:** Post-mortem file present + on-call sign-off in PR body.

### G5 — Compliance baseline

- **Threshold:** All seven security policies in `compliance/policies/` reviewed within the trailing 12 months. LGPD policy names the active DPO (not a placeholder). SOC 2 CC mapping (`compliance/audit-controls/soc2-cc.yaml`) has 100% `evidence_path` resolution.
- **Why:** "Public GA" implies enterprise prospects will request the trust pack. Missing artifacts at request time stretches the sales cycle by weeks.
- **Evidence:** `git log compliance/` + `compliance/audit-controls/coverage.txt` (generated by CI).

### G6 — Key rotation drill

- **Threshold:** First end-to-end key-rotation drill executed (minisign signing key + Stripe API key + Cloudflare tunnel token) per `compliance/runbooks/key-rotation.md`. Drill is logged in the runbook's drill log table.
- **Why:** GA assumes the team can rotate compromised credentials in hours, not days. The drill is the only proof.
- **Evidence:** Drill row in `compliance/runbooks/key-rotation.md`.

## Process

Quarterly review:

1. PM compiles a one-page evidence report citing each gate's evidence source.
2. PM circulates to TL + Rodrigo for sign-off.
3. Approvers either green-light (status stays GA), flag a breach (downgrade to "limited beta" with remediation owner + deadline), or set a follow-up review.
4. Result published to `compliance/audit-controls/ga-gate-history.md` (append-only).

Gate breach handling:

- **Hard breach** (G1, G2, G3) → public-facing status changes to "limited beta", new-customer signups paused, existing customers notified within 48 h.
- **Soft breach** (G4, G5, G6) → remediation plan posted within 72 h, GA status retained pending plan execution within 30 d.
